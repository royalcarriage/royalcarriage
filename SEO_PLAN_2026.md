## 2. On-Page SEO Strategy

### Title Tag Formula

```
[Primary Keyword] | [Location] | [Brand] - [Value Prop]
```

Examples:

- "Chicago Airport Limo Service | O'Hare & Midway | Royal Carriage - 24/7 Professional Transportation"
- "Wedding Limo Chicago | Bridal Party Transportation | Royal Carriage - Elegant Wedding Service"

### Meta Description Formula (150-160 chars)

```
[Primary benefit]. [Service details]. [CTA]. [Trust signal].
```

Examples:

- "Professional airport limo service to O'Hare & Midway. 24/7 availability, flight tracking, meet & greet. Book now. Rated 5 stars on Google."

### Header Structure (H1-H3)

```
H1: [Primary Keyword] (1 per page)
H2: [Service/Benefit Keyword] (3-5 per page)
H3: [Long-tail/Location Keywords] (5-10 per page)
```

### Image Optimization

- **File naming:** `{keyword}-{location}.webp` (e.g., `airport-limo-ohare-chicago.webp`)
- **Alt text:** Descriptive with keywords (e.g., "Black luxury sedan for O'Hare airport transportation")
- **WebP format:** 80% quality, max 200KB per image
- **Lazy loading:** All below-fold images
- **Responsive:** srcset for mobile/tablet/desktop
